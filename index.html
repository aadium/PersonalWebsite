A little bit about myself

My journey in the field of Biomedical Engineering started off in a very unusual way. Ever since I started high school, my intention was to make a career in the field of medicine, as a doctor. Very soon, I found myself attending doctor shadowing sessions, and I started gaining a deeper understanding of the role, and the procedures a doctor has to perform. At the same time, I gained interest in the techniques, machines, and equipment used by doctors, and very soon, I was in a dilemma, whether to choose medicine or biomedical engineering as a career option. Ultimately, I opted for Biomedical Engineering, as it embodies a synergy between engineering and medicine, utilizing engineering principles to create solutions for medical problems. My interest lies in the innovative and progressive nature of this field, resolving contemporary medical issues for the benefit of future generations.
